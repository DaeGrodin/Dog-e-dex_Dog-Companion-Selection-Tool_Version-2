About the Project

Dog E-dex is a basic visual introduction to dog breeds. It allows users to explore different dog breeds by looking at images, creating an easy way to learn about dogs.

Why I Chose This Project

Many people are unfamiliar with different dog breeds. Since people tend to choose dogs often by appearance, this app serves as an entry point to explore a variety of dog breeds visually and determine what appeals to them.

How It Can Be Used

The application is meant for casual browsing — click on a breed, view an image, and start identifying preferences. It can spark curiosity and guide future research into breed temperament and care.

Future Goals

In the future, I'd like to expand Dog E-dex by including more breed details — such as descriptions, temperaments, and suitability for different lifestyles. This way, users can make more informed decisions.
